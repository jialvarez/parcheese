### GAMES RULES ###

[R01] When you obtain 6, 3 times consecutively, move the last moved checker
  to home. (DONE)

[R02] If the player gets 5, and he has checkers at home, take out one of
  these automatically if it is possible. (DONE)

[R03] When a player gets 5, and he has checkers at home, but two of his 
  checkers are in the initial position, the player can't take out a checker 
  from home. (DONE)

[R04] When a player gets 5, and he has checkers at home, but there are one 
  of his checkers in the initial position and also another player's checker, 
  the checker of the opponent is moved to its home, and the player that gets 5
  move one checker across 20 squares. (DONE)

[R05] When a player moves one of his checkers to a square previously occupied
  by another player's checker he have to move one of his checkers (not 
  necessarily the last moved) 20 squares. (DONE)

[R06] When a player moves one of his checkers to a square previously occupied
  by any two checkers (same color), a barrier is created in this square and 
  none of the checkers can pass through it. (DONE)

[R07] When a player moves one of his checkers to a SECURE square previously
  occupied by two checkers of different color, the checker currently moving
  can jump over this NOT barrier. (DONE, check with UI)

[R08] When a checker arrives to its Nirvana the owner of this checker have 
  to move one of his other checkers 10 squares. (DONE)

[R09] When a player has all his checkers in the board if he gets a 6, he moves 
  one of his checkers 12 squares. (DONE)

[R10] When a player has a barrier somewhere in the board AND all his checkers
  are out of home AND he gets 6 in the dice (12 squares movement),
  the barrier automatically breaks and one of its checkers it will moved.
  (DONE)

[R11] When a player is in his turn, and none of their checkers can be moved,
  the player losts the turn. (DONE)

[R12] When a player select a checker than can not be moved, but exists
  another checker that can do it, he don't must lost his turn. (DONE)

[R13] When a player takes all their checkers to its Nirvana end the game.
